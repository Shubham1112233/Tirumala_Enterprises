Our Services

Swimming Pool / Waterbodies Waterproofing

Our specialized portfolio of multiple waterproofing projects ensures that we meet all your complex requirements at practical and cost-effective pricing.

Basement Waterproofing

Basement waterproofing is essential to protect below-ground structures from water ingress, soil pressure, and harsh environmental conditions. We use advanced systems like HDPE and SBS modified bitumen membranes for high durability, flexibility, and chemical resistance.

SBS Modified Bitumen Waterproofing

SBS waterproofing is a flexible, torch-applied membrane ideal for structures exposed to thermal movement. Its strength adapts to soil pressure and temperature fluctuations.

PU Coating for Podium

Podium waterproofing requires a seamless, flexible coating to handle structural movement, UV exposure, and environmental stressâ€”ensuring long-term durability.

Sunken Area Waterproofing

Bathrooms and kitchens are moisture-prone. Fast-curing compounds seal joints and corners quickly, making sunken waterproofing reliable and essential.

Terrace Waterproofing

Terraces face harsh conditions. PU coatings give seamless flexibility and resist UV and thermal changes, preventing seepage and structural damage.
