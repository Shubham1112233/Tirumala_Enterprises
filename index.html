Our Services

Industrial Waterproofing

It involves specialized systems designed to protect large-scale structures such as factories, warehouses, and plants from water ingress, chemical exposure, and environmental stress. These solutions are tailored to withstand heavy usage, structural movement, and harsh conditions, ensuring long-term durability and performance.

Basement Waterproofing

It is essential to protect below-ground structures from water ingress, soil pressure, and harsh environmental conditions. We use advanced systems like HDPE and SBS modified bitumen membranes that offer high durability, flexibility, and chemical resistance. These materials create a robust barrier against moisture while accommodating thermal movement and soil shifts, making them ideal for both deep basements and complex structural foundations.

PU Coating for Podium

Podium waterproofing requires a seamless, flexible coating to withstand structural movement and prevent water penetration, ensuring long-term durability. It also helps in protecting the surface from UV exposure, mechanical wear, and environmental stress.

Sunken Area Waterproofing Using Fast Flex

Sunken areas like bathrooms and kitchens are highly prone to water leakage due to constant moisture exposure, making proper waterproofing essential. Using fast-curing compounds ensures quicker turnaround and reliable sealing of hidden joints and corners.

Terrace Waterproofing with PU Coating

Terraces are constantly exposed to harsh weather conditions, making waterproofing crucial to avoid seepage and structural damage. PU coatings provide seamless protection, flexibility, and excellent resistance to UV and thermal variations.

Water Tank Waterproofing

Water tanks require reliable, long-lasting waterproofing to prevent leaks, protect structural integrity, and maintain water quality. Effective waterproofing also ensures hygiene by preventing contamination and microbial growth in potable water storage.
